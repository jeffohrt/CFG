Hot Chocolate

→ 4 cups milk

→ 1/2 cup grated semisweet chocolate

→ 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

→ 1 egg

→ melt chocolate into milk without boiling

→ whisk egg until smooth, mix in

→ simmer and whisk until thick, creamy and foamy

*** this can all be done in a microwave

→ 1/2 cup cocoa powder

→ 1/2 cup sugar

→ 4 cups milk

→ 1/2 cup of blend, heavy cream, whipping cream

→ 1 teaspoon or less of vanilla

→ mix, whisk while simmering, serve
