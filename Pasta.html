Chase's Pasta Salad

→ boil pasta to completion in heavily salted water

→ drain and cool

→ add equal parts Helmann's and Miracle Whip - to taste

→ add can(s) of flakes of tuna, chicken or turkey - to taste

→ add heat (red pepper flakes etc) to taste

→ chill and serve
